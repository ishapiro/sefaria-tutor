# Sefaria Tutor: Empowering Independent Learning of Jewish Texts
## A Guide for Jewish Educators

### Overview
Sefaria Tutor is an AI-powered study companion designed to help students transition from basic reading to independent textual mastery (*lekshir*). By combining the vast library of **Sefaria** with advanced Large Language Models, the app provides a personalized, word-by-word bridge to the original Hebrew and Aramaic sources.

---

### Current Features (Available Now)
The following features are currently live and ready for use in the classroom or for independent study:

*   **Full Sefaria Library Access:** Seamlessly browse Tanakh, Talmud, Liturgy, and more through a clean, modern interface.
*   **Contextual AI Translation:** Unlike generic translators, our AI is prompted as a "Torah Teacher," providing translations that respect the nuances of Jewish thought.
*   **Word-by-Word Grammatical Analysis:** Click any phrase to see a detailed breakdown of every word, including:
    *   **Shoresh (Root):** Identification of the lexical root.
    *   **Binyan:** Verb conjugation patterns (e.g., Pa'al, Piel).
    *   **Grammar Notes:** Explanations of prefixes, suffixes, and tense.
*   **Integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS):** 
    *   Listen to a full phrase to master the flow of the sentence.
    *   Click individual words to hear precise Hebrew/Aramaic pronunciation.
*   **Smart Translation Cache:** A community-driven "Dictionary" that remembers previously looked-up phrases, ensuring speed and consistency.
*   **Google & Email Authentication:** Secure accounts for students to manage their study sessions.

### Product Roadmap (Upcoming Features)
We are actively developing features to support the specific needs of Jewish Day Schools and High Schools:

#### 1. Student Skill Building
*   **Personalized Vocabulary Lists:** Students can save "trouble words" to a personal dictionary for review.
*   **Anki/Quizlet Export:** Turn saved words into flashcards with one click for homework or test prep.
*   **Root Explorer:** A visual tool to track how a single *shoresh* appears across different genres of text.

*   **Teacher Dashboards:** Leaders can view anonymized data on which verses or words their class is finding difficult, allowing for more focused lesson planning.
*   **Pinned Assignments:** Teachers can create "Direct Links" to specific chapters, so students land exactly where they need to study.
*   **Chavrusa Mode:** Collaborative digital study where students can share notes and lookups in real-time.

### Why Use Sefaria Tutor?
*   **Promotes Independence:** Students no longer get "stuck" on a single word; they have the tools to move forward on their own.
*   **Focuses on Grammar:** We don't just give the answer; we explain *why* the word means what it does by showing the root and structure.
*   **Free for All:** This project is provided free of charge to support the growth of Torah study worldwide.
